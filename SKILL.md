## Quick reference
- Tokens: `colors_and_type.css`
- Stickers + mascot + icons: `assets/`
- Component previews: `preview/`
- Reusable JSX components for the site: `ui_kits/site/`
- Voice & tone: lowercase, Vinglish (EN + VN), warm Gen Z, emoji as emotional punctuation
- Palette: pastel pink, cream, baby blue, lavender on cream paper; deep aubergine ink (never #000)
- Fonts: Pacifico (display), Quicksand (body), Caveat (handwritten)
